# Introduction

Welcome to Halcyon!

## What is Halcyon?

Embark on a thrilling adventure in the mystical world of Halcyon! As a skilled hunter and herbalist, you must save the forest of Aranthia from a deadly sickness caused by dark mages. But your journey is just beginning as you face treacherous ice fields and battle a sorceress in Glacier's Edge, explore an ancient temple in Celestial, and finally confront the source of great evil in the heart of Morvaine. With allies by your side and supernatural abilities at your fingertips, can you vanquish the enemy and become the Champion of Halcyon? Explore the unique biomes and cultures of the cities in this RPG, and experience a thrilling story filled with **Danger** and **Excitement**.
